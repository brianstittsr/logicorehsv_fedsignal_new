## Part 6: Certifications for Government/OEM Suppliers

### 6.1 Required Certifications Overview

For manufacturers seeking government and OEM contracts, the following certifications are critical:

#### Tier 1: Mandatory for DoD Contracts

| Certification | Description | Timeline | Cost Range |
|---------------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **CMMC Level 1** | Foundational cybersecurity practices | 30-60 days | $5K-$15K |
| **CMMC Level 2** | Protection of CUI (NIST 800-171) | 90-180 days | $15K-$50K |
| **CMMC Level 3** | Advanced protection against APTs | 6-12 months | $50K-$150K |
| **SPRS Score** | Supplier Performance Risk System | Ongoing | Self-assessment |

#### Tier 2: Quality & Process Standards

| Certification | Industry | Value Proposition |
|---------------|----------|-------------------|
| **ISO 9001:2015** | Universal | Quality management standard |
| **IATF 16949** | Automotive | Automotive quality standard |
| **AS9100** | Aerospace | Aerospace quality management |
| **ISO 13485** | Medical | Medical device quality |

#### Tier 3: Specialized Certifications

| Certification | Sector | Competitive Advantage |
|---------------|--------|----------------------|
| **ITAR Registration** | Defense | Export-controlled items |
| **FAA Certification** | Aerospace | Flight-critical components |
| **FDA Registration** | Medical | Medical device manufacturing |
| **NIST SP 800-171** | Government | Cybersecurity compliance |

### 6.2 OEM-Specific Requirements

**Automotive OEMs (GM, Ford, Stellantis):**
- IATF 16949 mandatory
- PPAP (Production Part Approval Process)
- Specific sustainability requirements
- Cybersecurity requirements (ISO/SAE 21434)

**Aerospace OEMs (Boeing, Lockheed, Raytheon):**
- AS9100/AS9100D
- NADCAP certifications (specific processes)
- ITAR compliance
- CMMC Level 2+ (increasingly required)

**Defense Primes (General Dynamics, Northrop):**
- CMMC Level 2 minimum (trending to Level 3)
- NIST 800-171 compliance
- DFARS 252.204-7012
- SPRS score above acceptable threshold

**Medical Device OEMs (Medtronic, Stryker):**
- ISO 13485
- FDA registration
- ISO 14971 (risk management)
- Cybersecurity requirements (increasing)

**Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)**

**Phase 2: Core Certification (Months 4-9)**
